Grass-Fed Filet Mignon Steak Recipe

Grass-Fed Filet Mignon Steak:

  • 2 grass-fed filet mignon steaks (6-8 ounces each)
  • 2 tablespoons grass-fed ghee
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste

Season the filet mignon steaks generously with salt and pepper. In a large skillet, heat 1 tablespoon of grass-fed ghee over medium-high heat. When the ghee is hot, add the steaks to the skillet. Sear the steaks for about 3-4 minutes on each side for medium-rare, adjusting the time based on your desired level of doneness. Remove the steaks from the skillet and let them rest for a few minutes before serving.

Kale, Fig, and Green Onion Salad:

  • 4-6 large kale leaves, stems removed and leaves thinly sliced
  • 4-6 ripe figs, quartered
  • 4 green onions, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste

In a large mixing bowl, combine the thinly sliced kale, quartered figs, and sliced green onions. In a small bowl, whisk together the extra-virgin olive oil, balsamic vinegar, salt, and pepper to create the dressing. Drizzle the dressing over the kale, figs, and green onions in the mixing bowl. Toss the salad gently to coat the ingredients evenly with the dressing.

Sweet Potato Mash with Cinnamon:

  • 2 large sweet potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 2 tablespoons grass-fed ghee
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste

Place the sweet potato cubes in a large pot of boiling water and cook until they are fork-tender, about 15-20 minutes. Drain the sweet potatoes and return them to the pot. Add 1 tablespoon of grass-fed ghee, ground cinnamon, salt, and pepper to taste. Mash the sweet potatoes until they are smooth and well combined. Keep warm.

To Serve:

Arrange the sliced filet mignon steak on individual plates as the main dish. Serve the Kale, Fig, and Green Onion Salad as one side dish alongside the steak. Serve the Sweet Potato Mash with Cinnamon as the second side dish.

This complete meal features a perfectly seared Grass-Fed Filet Mignon Steak, a refreshing Kale, Fig, and Green Onion Salad, and a comforting Sweet Potato Mash with Cinnamon. Enjoy the combination of flavors and textures!
